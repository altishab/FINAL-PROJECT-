About Me

My name is Altin Shab and I am a keyboard enthusiast. I have been collecting and using keyboards for over 10 years and have gained a lot of knowledge and experience in the field. I have tried many different brands and styles of keyboards, and I have a particular interest in mechanical keyboards. I enjoy the tactile feedback and precision that they offer, and I love the wide range of customization options that are available. In addition to my keyboard collection, I also have a large library of books and resources on the subject of keyboard design and technology.

About My Keyboard Company

My keyboard company, Qwerty, is a small business that I started in 2020. We specialize in designing and manufacturing high-quality, hand-crafted mechanical keyboards. Our keyboards are known for their durability, versatility, and beautiful design. We use only the finest materials and components in our keyboards, and each one is built to last. In addition to our standard line of keyboards, we also offer custom design and manufacturing services for customers who want a truly unique keyboard. We have a team of experienced designers and craftsmen who are dedicated to creating the perfect keyboard for each individual customer. In addition to our focus on quality and customization, we are also committed to providing excellent customer service. We want our customers to be completely satisfied with their purchase, and we are always happy to answer any questions or concerns that they may have.
